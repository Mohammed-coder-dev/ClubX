Omani Student Organization at Penn (OSO Penn)

Club Mission

The Omani Student Organization at Penn is a vibrant community of Omani students enrolled in the English Language Program (ELP) at Fisher Bennett Hall. Our primary goal is to promote and celebrate the rich and diverse aspects of Omani culture through various activities. We firmly believe that cultural exchange is crucial for building bridges of understanding between Omanis and the Penn community.

Our organization is committed to providing a platform for our fellow students and the wider community to learn more about Oman's history, traditions, and way of life. We organize various events such as cultural festivals, lectures, and workshops that showcase the unique and fascinating aspects of Omani culture. These events are open to all interested in learning more about Oman and its people.

Our organization strives to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where all students can feel at home and make new friends.
